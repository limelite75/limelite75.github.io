Do you and your child like hopping, dancing and excercising together while listening to happy music? Family Zumba is for you!

Children need to move to stay healthy. Excercise has to be fun! The same is applicable for adults. It is difficult as a parent of young children to get the time to exercise. After a full time job and taking care of the household you also want time to socialize with your kids. Family Zumba is a fun way to spend time with your child and exercise together.

Me and my 5-year old are working on putting together a choreo that will make both children and adults have fun and get a good exercise together.

Target group is 4-6 year-olds with a parent. But you know your child best. Perhaps your 3-year old likes to follow simple choreography. Perhaps your 7 year old wants to spend Sunday morning doing an activity together with you. You are welcome!
